DESTINATION

The Swissotel Tbilisi is located in the city center, walking distance from the old town, meaning guests can walk into and around the city centre for attractions, parks and historical landmarks. Enjoy first-class luxury and Swiss hospitality in stylishly modern guest rooms and suites that surpass all expectations, with stunning City views. The Swissotel Tbilisi is situated right next to some of the main Tbilisi tourist attractions, such as the Bazari Orbeliani, the Parliament building, the Galleria shopping mall and nearby you can visit Dry Bridge Flea Market.
